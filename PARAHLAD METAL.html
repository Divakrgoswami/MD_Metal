Electric Rickshaw

This array of Electric Rickshaws promotes the concept of safe and enjoyable riding without causing environmental pollution. Offered in different design choice, this range of vehicles has seating facility for maximum 5 passengers. These can cover 40km/hour without causing jerk. These can be availed with back side covering and opening design based option. Several models of these Electric Rickshaws are equipped with luggage carrying space on their roof. These specific models have stainless steel railing on rooftop to avoid accidental fall of transported baggage. Offered in different color choices, these battery powered vehicles are acknowledged for their comfortable seating facility. Seat handles and upper handles of these transportation solutions are meant for safe and jerk free riding experience. Four spoke wheels of these vehicles evenly distribute their weight in order to maintain balance while driving on rough road surface. Required seating space optimizes comfort of passengers.

Feature:-


  1. Provided vehicles have 40km/h mileage and these have seating arrangement for maximum five passengers.
  2. Seats of these vehicles are cushioned
  3. Jerk free movement, low noise generation, pollution free driving mode
  4. Hand brake of these vehicles is impact proof.
